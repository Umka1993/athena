About Us

Our priority is to help our clients cope with family problems by directing them towards a peaceful resolution.

While issues related to family law can have drastic effects on your future, these can be minimized if managed appropriately.

Family lawyers at Athena have experience in addressing the following legal issues:

  • Separation or divorce
  • Child custody and access
  • Family Justice Services
  • Child protection and adoptions
  • Division or equalization of family property
  • Support for you, your spouse, and children
  • Treatment of a matrimonial home
  • Enforcement of support payments

We provide family law services nation wide.

The initial consultation is free of charge. Whatever your legal issue is, you will feel calmer and more confident after discussing it with a legal specialist and developing a strategy for the protection of your legal interests.

Our Services

Separation
or divorce
Athena's team always aims at amicable and agreeable separations to avoid trial. However, in cases that don't seem to be resolved amicably, we do everything we can to protect our client's interests in court.
Child protection
and adoptions
Adoption is a complex process that differs according to the type of adoption, where the child is from, variances in state laws, and other factors. Therefore, it's best to consult with a family law attorney.
Enforcement of
support payments
No matter what circumstances are, children must have support and protection. Athena Group offers the most competent and experienced lawyers to help our clients protect their families and children.
Child custody
and access
Child custody and access cases are usually a part of larger divorce cases. If the parents' interests don't coincide, we know what to do — try every possible resource to meet our clients' needs and protect their rights.
